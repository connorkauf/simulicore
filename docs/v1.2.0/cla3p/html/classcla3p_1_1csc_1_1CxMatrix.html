The copy assignment operator.

Replaces the contents of (*this) with those of other, other is destroyed. If (*this) is empty, constructs a matrix with a copy of the contents of other, other is unchanged.
If (*this) is not empty, performs a deep copy of the data of other to (*this). The sizes, non-zeros and property of (*this) should match those of other.
